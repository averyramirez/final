BLANK BEAUTY

Blank Beauty was created in 2023 by our founder, Avery Ramirez. After working in the cosmetics industry for a decade and seeing how complicated it was for consumers to choose skincare products, she set out to formulate a brand that simplified the experience.

Our mission is simple: provide clean, effective, and approachable skincare. We were able to curate two collections that covered all of our bases, leaving no customer behind in the process.
